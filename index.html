About.

Encrypt Numbers was born in Paris. City of light and luxury. Encrypt Numbers aims to bring luxury to the metaverse but also to reality.

The NFT are unique, the clothes must be too.

Encrypt numbers wants to combine the physical world and the metaverse while bringing the luxury side to it.

Encrypt Numbers is a brand at the frontier between the physical and digital world.

Intended to offer our holders unique pieces in the real world but also owned in the form of NFT for exclusive access to the following collections.

We want to incorporate that luxury rating that it lacks. For this, in addition to the physical coins, the integration with the metaverse is an important step for Encrypt Numbers. The Encrypt coin will allow to buy directly the NFT Encrypt Numbers within the metaverse.

At the end of the first year, we are going to gather Encrypt Numbers coin holders because they are the ones who will decide the next actions of the brand within the metaverse.
